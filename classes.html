Classes

Children's Dance

(ages 2-4)

Children's dance is the perfect start into the world of dance for younglings. Children will begin to learn how to move to music and will learn simple step combinations through the use of games.

Ballet

(ages 4+)

Our ballet classes use traditional classical ballet steps and classical music. We have 7 different levels of classes that students work their way through according to age and skill level.

Jazz

(ages 4+)

Jazz dance combines African and European dance styles to create a style that combines sharp and fluid movements. Our jazz classes are full of funk and are divided up according to participants' ages.

Modern

(ages 8+)

Modern is a form of dance that includes fluid free style, often to unique instrumental music. Modern is a great style for dancers who have taken jazz and ballet classes and are looking for more of a combination of the two.

Contemporary

(ages 8+)

Contemporary dance is a fluid form of dance that focuses heavily on expression. Slower, modern songs are often used for contemporary dance. Contemporary is a great style for those who love ballet but would like to try a more free and fluid form of dance.

Hip Hop

(ages 8+)

Hip hop is a range of street dance styles primarily performed to hip hop music. It is vibrant and full of freestyle.

Show-Dance

(ages 13+)

Show dance is a form of dance that combines a form of stunts and dance moves to create an entertaining show for the audience. In this class, you will learn numerous acrobatic moves, including aerials and flips. Intermediate dance experience is required for this class.

Yoga

(ages 16+)

Yoga is offered for those looking for some extra stretching or relaxation at the end of a busy day. Our yoga classes focus on stretching and flows.

Stretching

(ages 10+)

Our stretching classes are for anyone who wants to improve their flexibility. We highly recommend this class for our intermediate to advanced dancers.
